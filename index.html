Faculty External Partnership Toolkit

School of Engineering

Development of this toolkit was supported by NSF EPIIC Award #2432471.

The goal of this work is to support School of Engineering faculty researchers in navigating external partnership and grant proposal processes with greater ease and efficiency. These interactive tools aim to reduce the burden on faculty PIs by providing templates with automated calculations, consistent formatting, and compliance with funding agency requirements.This toolkit is designed to help meet two focus areas of the NSF EPIIC grant: building institutional capacity for sustainable external partnerships and equipping faculty with accessible resources to initiate and manage research collaborations effectively .
